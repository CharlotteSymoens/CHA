ART ROUTE AT WAREGEM: 

REPETITION IN HABIT AND MEMORY 

 2017

A female group exhibition in the Verde Art Gallery, a circular space which influenced our movements. As LOUCHE we did 12 different continuous acts, 20 minutes each. From the opening until closing time. We brought our friend Victor Van Wassenhove to take an instant picture of every action, which he then placed in the vitrine underneath every drawing that fits the action. The end result was a sculpture of musical instruments, drawings, fruit, skateboards, â€¦ an artefact of everything we used during those four hours.
