BANG! #5 curated by Joris van de Moortel - 2017

A festival organised in the Begijnhofkerk, a church in Brussels. During 6 weeks Joris chose 12 different curators to fill an evening of artistic practices, mostly music/sound related. The theme of this edition was inspired by a painting of Bruegel De Oude: 12 Spreuken/ 12 Spells. We got invited by Gerard Herman and Sarah Yu Zeebroek. Every evening had a different tonality.

Our first evening was called: In de ene hand vuur, in de andere water dragen / Carrying water in one hand, fire in the other hand in E. A church is from an acoustics point of view a very characteristic building because of its natural reverb. We guided everyone outside the church and all got a little book with instructions. First there was a ceremonial warm up with a glass of tea per person. In the second part the group was divided in two so Ma√Øly and me had both our choir to lead. The public was our human instrument. Some people enjoyed the singing others hated it. Mostly it sounded harmonious, sometimes terrible.

The second evening: Zich onder een blauwe huik verbergen / To hide under a blue hood in Bb. Talking lyrics is making confessions without shame, so we rocked the confessional chairs with two songs. One about the unconditional love you get from your dog and the other one of someone living all his/her life in his/her living room.
