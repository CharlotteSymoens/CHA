LOUCHE,
a duo I form with Maily Beyrens, we work time-based and mostly it becomes a concert we perform with a personal score each of us makes in advance. We never did the same show twice, maybe one day we will but we like to adapt one show to a particular event in a specific place. We work with all kind of improfessional instruments that fit our state of mind. This collaboration gives me a lot of energy and we vibrate on a similar level of consciousness that feeds our inspiration together.
